Privacy Policy for My Daily Expense

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

My Daily Expense is an offline expense tracking application designed to help users manage their finances securely. This app respects your privacy and does not collect personal data.

Data Collection

This application does NOT collect, store, or share personal information.

  • 100% Offline
  • No account required
  • No analytics tracking
  • No cloud storage
  • No personal data collection

Offline Usage

All financial data entered into the app is stored locally on your device. Your financial data never leaves your phone.

Permissions

The app may request storage access only for exporting or importing CSV files created by the user.

Data Security

Your data remains on your device and is protected by your device’s security system.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in future versions of the application.
